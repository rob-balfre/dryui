# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

DryUI is for engineers, designers who code, and AI-assisted builders working in Svelte or SvelteKit. They are often inside an editor with an agent open, trying to get from a prompt to usable production UI without correcting the same component, token, and accessibility mistakes repeatedly.

## Product Purpose

DryUI gives agents rails: a component library, CLI, MCP server, lint layer, theme controls, and live feedback loop that reduce choice and make the right implementation path obvious. The public site should make the product feel like an instrument for controlled generation, not another prompt-to-mockup toy.

## Brand Personality

Precise, terse, and kinetic. DryUI should feel like a workshop jig, a terminal diagnostic, and a component catalog in one surface.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic dark SaaS gradients, centered hero stacks, purple AI branding, oversized icon card grids, and mockup-only generator language. Avoid editorial magazine styling because DryUI is a developer tool, not a publication.

## Design Principles

Show the loop: ask, build, check, mark up, repeat.

Make constraints visible. Layout grids, rails, checks, and signals should carry the identity.

Prefer production proof over vibes. Components, tokens, lint, and MCP are the story.

Use strong type and sparse copy. The brand should sound confident without sounding inflated.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast, preserve keyboard navigation, provide descriptive page titles, honor reduced motion, and avoid color-only meaning in workflow states.
